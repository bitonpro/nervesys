# Sentinel Design System

## Colors
- **Brand Primary**: `#00C16A` (BitOn Green)
- **Secondary/Dark**: `#1E2327`
- **Success**: `green-500`
- **Warning**: `amber-500`
- **Error**: `red-500`
- **Neutral**: Zinc (`#FAFAFA` to `#09090B`)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Heebo (300, 400, 500, 700)
- **Secondary Font**: Rubik

## Direction
- Native RTL support (Hebrew first).

## Logo
- Hexagon shield with a central node representing the device connection.
