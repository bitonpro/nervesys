User said "אתה תחליט מה הכי טוב" (you decide what's best) when presented with options.

**Why:** User manages many systems and doesn't want to micromanage every decision.
**How to apply:** For infrastructure decisions (which service to keep, how to fix conflicts), analyze the situation and take the best action. Only ask when truly ambiguous or destructive.
